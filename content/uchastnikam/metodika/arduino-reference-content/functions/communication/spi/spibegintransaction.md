**Description**

Initializes the SPI bus. Note that calling [SPI.begin()](../begin) is required before calling this one.

**Syntax**

`SPI.beginTransaction(mySettings)`

**Parameters**

mySettings: the chosen settings (see [SPISettings](../spisettings)).

**Returns**

None.
